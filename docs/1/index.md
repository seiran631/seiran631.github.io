## If You Would Be My Friend

----

#### Author: [Toraneko](https://mypage.syosetu.com/691108/)

#### Link: [https://ncode.syosetu.com/n4283il/](https://ncode.syosetu.com/n4283il/)

----

On the way back after defeating the Demon Lord, I asked the Saintess who journeyed with me to be my friend.

And I was met with a harsh rejection.

"Because I dislike you."

Anger, hatred, disdain.

I realized I didn't know anything about her.

Not her birth, not her upbringing, nor what lies ahead of her...
